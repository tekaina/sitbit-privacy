SitBit Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 5, 2026

SitBit is a habit-building app for adults who spend long hours sitting. It helps users build healthier routines with desk exercises, movement reminders, snack tracking, streaks, quests, and group sports session features. SitBit is intended for users aged 18 and older.

Delete Your Account & Data

To request deletion of your SitBit account and all associated data:

  1. Open the app and go to Settings → Delete Account.
  2. If you no longer have the app installed, email support@sitbit.co with the subject Delete my SitBit account.
  3. Include the email address used for your account and, if possible, the approximate date you created it.

We will permanently delete your account and all associated personal data within 30 days of your request. Some data may be retained longer only where required by applicable law (for example, financial records or fraud prevention obligations), and only for the duration required by law.

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how SitBit ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, stores, and shares information when you use the SitBit mobile application and related services. By using SitBit, you agree to the practices described in this policy. We only collect data needed to provide core app features and improve the user experience.

2. Data We Collect

  • Account information: name, email address, and profile details from Google Sign-In or email/password registration.
  • App activity: exercises completed, streaks, quests, post-game ratings, session participation, and related timestamps.
  • Health and fitness information: snack logs, water tracking, movement activity, and habit progress you manually enter.
  • Location data: approximate and/or precise device location, only if you grant permission, to support nearby games and related app features.
  • Device and diagnostic data: app interaction logs, crash reports, and device identifiers used for app functionality and push notifications.
  • Support requests: any information you voluntarily send us by email.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • Provide, operate, and personalize SitBit features.
  • Save and sync your progress across devices.
  • Support account creation, login, authentication, and verification.
  • Send push notifications, reminders, and account-related emails.
  • Show nearby games and group session features when location is enabled.
  • Respond to support, deletion, and correction requests.
  • Diagnose technical issues and improve app stability.

4. How We Share Your Data

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data only with the following categories of recipients:

  • Service providers: Google Firebase is used for authentication, cloud storage, push messaging, and app analytics. Data shared with Firebase is governed by Google's privacy policies.
  • Legal obligations: We may disclose data if required by law, court order, or to protect the rights, safety, or property of SitBit or its users.
  • Business transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, your data may be transferred. We will notify users before personal data is transferred and becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

5. Third-Party Services

SitBit uses the following third-party services. Your use of these services is also subject to their respective privacy practices:

  • Firebase Privacy & Security
  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Google Terms of Service

6. Location Permissions

If you enable location features, SitBit may collect approximate or precise location to support nearby games and similar functionality. Location data is only used while the feature is active and is not shared with advertisers. You can disable location permissions at any time in your device settings; some features will stop working if you do.

7. Data Retention & Deletion

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to provide SitBit services. The table below describes our standard retention periods by data type:

Data Type Retention Period Reason
Account information (name, email) Until account deletion + 30 days Required to operate your account
App activity (streaks, quests, ratings) Until account deletion + 30 days Core app functionality and history
Health & fitness logs Until account deletion + 30 days User-controlled wellness tracking
Location data Not persistently stored; used in real time only Nearby game discovery only
Crash and diagnostic logs Up to 90 days App stability and debugging
Support emails Up to 12 months after resolution Customer support quality
Legal/fraud-related records As required by applicable law Legal obligation

When you delete your account, all personal data in the categories above (except legally required records) is permanently removed from our systems within 30 days. After deletion is complete, your data cannot be recovered.

To request deletion, see the Delete Your Account & Data section above.

8. Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data, including secure encrypted transmission (HTTPS/TLS), Firebase security rules, and access controls. No method of storage or transmission is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security. We will notify affected users if a data breach affecting their personal data occurs, in accordance with applicable law.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data as described above.
  • Restriction / Objection: Request we limit or stop processing your data in certain circumstances.
  • Data portability: Request a copy of your data in a structured, machine-readable format where technically feasible.
  • Permissions: Revoke permissions such as location at any time through your device settings.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@sitbit.co. We will respond within 30 days.

10. Children's Privacy

SitBit is intended for users aged 18 years and older. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 18. If we become aware that a child under 18 has provided us with personal data, we will delete it promptly. If you believe a minor has used our app, please contact us at support@sitbit.co.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the "Last updated" date above and may notify you through the app, by email, or through other appropriate means. Continued use of SitBit after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
